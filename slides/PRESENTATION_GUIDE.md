# MedRaC Scientific Seminar Guide

The main deck contains 32 slides: 26 paper-focused slides, three local-demo slides, and three discussion/closing slides. Appendix slides follow the closing slide and should be opened only when needed.

## Suggested pacing

- Slides 1-6: 5 minutes - motivation, gap, and contributions.
- Slides 7-14: 8 minutes - step-wise evaluation framework.
- Slides 15-19: 5 minutes - MedRaC methodology.
- Slides 20-26: 9 minutes - experimental design and findings.
- Slides 27-29: 4 minutes - local demo and mini-experiment.
- Slides 30-32: 3 minutes - limitations, conclusions, and discussion.

## Presentation cautions

- Do not describe the ten-case local result as a reproduction or as statistically significant.
- State explicitly that local LLM step-wise evaluation was disabled; F/E/C/A, strict, CC, and FE metrics are unavailable.
- Distinguish equation-based and rule-based results before interpreting gains.
- Treat Table 5's printed `46.49%` value as table-reported; the conflicting `7.34%` prose is disclosed in the appendix.
- The seminar safe-execution layer reduces risk but is not a secure sandbox.
- The paper and local implementation are research demonstrations, not clinical decision systems.
